You must read the drafts with a ruthless editorial eye, comparing them against the K.D. Hughes Style Guide.
Terminal Intensity: Eradicate any "social media fluff." No upbeat emojis, breezy tone, or generic marketing speak. Maintain "Manic Gravity."
High-Low Collision: Ensure every post mixes academic/philosophical terms with visceral, physical imagery (e.g., blood, sweat, metal, dirt).

</style_guide_audit>
